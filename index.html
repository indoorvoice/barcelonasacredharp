Sacred Harp

What it is

Community folk-singing of traditional music.

  • No auditions, rehearsals or performances.
  • No money or experience needed.
  • Friendly, inclusive community open to all.

We show up, we sing, we hang out. That's it.

When & Where

Thursdays, 7 - 9, MOB, Bailèn

  • We meet in Bar Bailén at 7 and sing downstairs.
  • Contact us if you have any questions!
  • If you arrive late, we're downstairs - text us!
